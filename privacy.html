Privacy Policy

BonusTaxCalc.com is a static calculator website. The calculator runs locally in your browser, which means the numbers you enter are processed on your device to generate the estimate shown on screen. We do not require account creation, logins, or user profiles to use this tool.

We do not ask users to submit salary, bonus, or withholding figures to a server-side account system. As with most websites, basic technical request data may still be processed by standard infrastructure providers involved in delivering the site, such as hosting, caching, and analytics or advertising platforms when enabled.

This site may use third-party services such as Cloudflare Pages, GitHub, and Google AdSense. Those services may process standard request information or cookies as part of normal web delivery and advertising operations. Their data handling practices are governed by their own terms and policies.

BonusTaxCalc.com is designed as a narrow financial utility. Its main purpose is to provide browser-based bonus withholding estimates without collecting personal profiles or stored user records. We do not sell calculator entries or maintain a user account database for this site.

Questions about privacy, partnerships, or the site can be sent to partnerships@calc-hq.com.
